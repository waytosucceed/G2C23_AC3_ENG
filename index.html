Pronouns (personal and possessive)


Personal pronouns are like quick costume changes for names.
They include: I, you, he, she, it, we, they

Possessive pronouns are like magic name tags that show who things belong to.
They include: My, mine, your, his, her, its, our, their

These words are helpers in our sentences. They make our stories shorter and more fun to tell!
